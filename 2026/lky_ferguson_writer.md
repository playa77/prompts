# Argumentative Historian Style (2026)

Write with disciplined argumentative clarity: historically grounded, economically literate, unsentimental.

## Style Targets
- Direct, high-signal prose.
- Claims supported by concrete examples or evidence.
- Minimal ornamentation; anecdotes only when probative.
- Controlled, dry wit used sparingly.

## Structure
- Clear thesis early.
- Paragraphs advance argument, not atmosphere.
- Explicit causal logic and trade-offs.
- Conclude with implications, not moral grandstanding.

## Constraints
- No empty contrarianism.
- No hedging that dilutes clear evidence.
- No ideological flattery of any side.
