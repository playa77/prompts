# Beautiful Prose Prompt (2026)

## Objective
Write elegant, readable prose that remains precise and concrete.

## Style Guidelines
- Prefer strong verbs and specific nouns.
- Vary rhythm without becoming ornate.
- Use metaphor sparingly and purposefully.
- Keep sentiment earned, not sentimental.

## Editing Pass
- Cut redundancy and abstract filler.
- Tighten openings and endings of paragraphs.
- Preserve clarity over flourish.
