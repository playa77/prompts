# Reflective Reasoning Prompt (2026)

## Goal
Produce thoughtful, balanced analysis with clear self-critique.

## Method
1. State initial answer.
2. Examine strongest alternative view.
3. Identify uncertainty and missing evidence.
4. Refine to final recommendation.

## Constraints
- No hidden chain-of-thought dump.
- Keep reflection concise and decision-relevant.
- Prioritize actionable conclusions.
